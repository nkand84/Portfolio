About me

Hello World !
I am Nitya Kandukuri, a Full Stack Web Developer, living in sunny San Diego, California. I recently graduated from the Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp at UCSD where I acquired hands on experience on various web based technologies.

The passion for web development happened by taking my favourite course, "Human Computer Interfaces" during my Masters'. One thing led to another and I landed my first job as a junior web developer at my university. These fortunate turn of events sparked my inner interest towards Web development. After my graduation, I started my career as a Front End Developer at the Electrical Engineering department, UNT. I was actively involved from the start to finish of the architecture of the project with the amazing Central Web Maintenance Team at UNT. When I am not coding, I enjoy spending time with my family, hiking, and photography.
